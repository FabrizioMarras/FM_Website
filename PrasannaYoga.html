Prasanna Yoga

THE CLIENT: Prasanna Yoga is a Yoga practice studio situated in the northern side of the city of Rotterdam, The Netherlands.

Web Design

The client was looking for a simple and fresh look for their Yoga school, where customers could find the right and necessary information to be able to attend their favourite yoga class. The design followed strict recommendations from the client, who provided a logo, color palette, images, content and full structure of the website, including a fully translated version of the site content.

After gathering all the necessary and given information, and assets, I created the final version of the project using Adobe Muse, including a Dutch translated version of the website and I embedded google maps linking to the yoga classroom location, contact links, in a mobile device friendly and responsive layout.
